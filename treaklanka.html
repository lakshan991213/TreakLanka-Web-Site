I had the most amazing 3 week trip ever to Sri Lanka (November 2008) . The island really does have so much to offer - nature, amazing beaches and countryside, beautiful wildlife, the most incredibly interesting historical and religious sites, wonderful food, good choice of accommodation to suit all budgets... I could go on and on! Undoubtedly the best way to see the country is to hire a guide and/or driver - it's the fastest way to cover a lot of ground and see all the sites but more importantly, it's how you are most likely to gain a true insight into the Sri Lankan way of life and the lovely Sinhalese people! I spent much of my time with Saminda Kumara, a guide I met in Kandy. Together with Indu, a fantastic driver, we travelled around much of the country and had so many good times on the way. Although we also used trains and buses a fair amount of time too (as they are quite an experience, super cheap, and not to be missed!). One of the most memorable evenings was in a little hut on a beach in the south, sharing a huge barbecued fish, drinking, making music and singing with a bunch of fishermen who were about to head off to sea for 3 months!
